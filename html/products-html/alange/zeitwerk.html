The A. Lange & Söhne Zeitwerk Honeygold Lumen (Reference 142.055) is a remarkable timepiece that combines three exceptional features: a jumping numerals display, luminous numerals, and a case made of 18-carat Honeygold. This limited edition watch is exclusively available in A. Lange & Söhne boutiques.

Design and Features:

  • Case: 41.9 mm diameter, 12.6 mm thickness, crafted from 18-carat Honeygold, a proprietary gold alloy known for its unique hue and durability. The case back is made of sapphire crystal, providing a view of the movement.
  • Dial: Semi-transparent sapphire crystal dial with black rhodiumed German silver time bridge. The luminous jumping numerals are visible at all times, enhancing readability in low-light conditions.
  • Movement: Manual-winding caliber L043.9, consisting of 462 parts, featuring a constant-force escapement and a power-reserve indicator. It offers a power reserve of 72 hours and operates at a frequency of 18,000 semi-oscillations per hour (2.5 Hz).
  • Strap: Dark brown alligator leather strap with an 18-carat Honeygold prong buckle, ensuring both comfort and elegance.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 41.9mm
  • Case Material: 18k pink (rose) gold
  • Dial: Semi-transparent sapphire crystal dial with black rhodiumed German silver time bridge.
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: A. Lange & Söhne Caliber L043.9 (manual wind), consisting of 462 parts.
  • Power Reserve: 72 hours
  • Frequency: 18,600 vph (2.5 Hz)
  • Strap: Dark brown alligator leather strap with an 18-carat Honeygold prong buckle.
  • Water Resistance: 30 meters
  • Edition: Limited to 200 pieces
