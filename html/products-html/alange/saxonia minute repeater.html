The A. Lange & Söhne Minute Repeater Perpetual (Reference 607.091FE) is an exceptional timepiece that combines two of the most sophisticated horological complications: a minute repeater and a perpetual calendar. This limited edition, crafted in 950 platinum, is restricted to just 50 pieces, underscoring its exclusivity. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Design and Features:

  • Case: Measures 40.5 mm in diameter and 12.1 mm in thickness, made of 950 platinum, providing a luxurious and durable casing.
  • Dial: Features a black enamel dial crafted in-house, with white gold sub-dials indicating day, date, month, leap year, and moon phase, all encircled by a white gold lesene.
  • Strap: Comes with a hand-stitched black alligator leather strap, fastened with a platinum deployant buckle, ensuring both comfort and elegance.

Movement:

Equipped with the manually wound caliber L122.2 movement, consisting of 640 parts. It offers a power reserve of 72 hours and operates at a frequency of 2.75 Hz (19,800 semi-oscillations per hour). The movement is visible through the sapphire crystal case back, showcasing the intricate craftsmanship.
