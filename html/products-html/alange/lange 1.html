The A. Lange & Söhne Lange 1 in rose gold with a blue dial (Ref. 191.063) is a limited-edition masterpiece, introduced in 2024 to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the Lange 1 collection. Limited to just 300 pieces, this elegant timepiece combines precision watchmaking with timeless design.

The watch features a 38.5mm 18-carat pink gold case and a deep blue dial, showcasing the signature off-center time display, large date window, and power reserve indicator. A blue alligator leather strap with a matching rose gold prong buckle adds a refined finishing touch.

Powered by the in-house L121.1 manual movement, the Lange 1 delivers a 72-hour power reserve and is beautifully displayed through a sapphire exhibition caseback.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 38.5mm
  • Case Material: 18k pink (rose) gold
  • Dial: Blue with off-center time, big date, and power reserve indicator
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: A. Lange & Söhne Caliber L121.1 (manual wind)
  • Power Reserve: 72 hours
  • Frequency: 21,600 vph (3 Hz)
  • Strap: Blue alligator leather with pink gold prong buckle
  • Water Resistance: 30 meters
  • Edition: Limited to 300 pieces
