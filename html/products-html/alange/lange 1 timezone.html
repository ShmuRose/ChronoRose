The A. Lange & Söhne Lange 1 Time Zone (Reference 136.032) is a sophisticated timepiece designed for frequent travelers and cosmopolites, offering dual time zone functionality alongside the brand's signature complications.

The watch features a 41.9mm 18-carat pink gold case. The argenté-colored dial is made from solid silver, adorned with applied pink gold hour markers and hands. It displays home time prominently, with a smaller sub-dial indicating a second time zone. Day/night indicators for both time zones are centrally positioned, and a day/night indicator for the second time zone is located at 5 o'clock. A city ring allows for easy adjustment of the second time zone, accommodating daylight saving time changes.

Powered by the in-house L141.1 manual movement,movement comprises 448 parts, offering a power reserve of 72 hours and operating at a frequency of 21,600 semi-oscillations per hour (3 Hz).

Equipped with a hand-stitched brown alligator leather strap, it is secured with an 18-carat pink gold prong buckle, ensuring both comfort and elegance.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 41.9mm
  • Case Material: 18k pink (rose) gold
  • Dial: Argenté-colored dial made from solid silver, with applied pink gold hour markers and hands. The sub-dial shows the second time zone with day/night indicators for both time zones.
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: A. Lange & Söhne Caliber L141.1 (manual wind)
  • Power Reserve: 72 hours
  • Frequency: 21,600 vph (3 Hz)
  • Strap: Hand-stitched brown alligator leather strap, secured with an 18-carat pink gold prong buckle.
  • Water Resistance: 30 meters
