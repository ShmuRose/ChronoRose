Patek Philippe Aquanaut

Reference: 5968A

945,000.00 NIS

×

Contact Us

The sporty and very contemporary Aquanaut collection welcomes its first chronograph – a complication that perfectly matches the character of this youthful and dynamic timepiece line. Endowed with a self-winding flyback chronograph movement, the Reference 5968 also stands out with remarkable aesthetic appeal, typified by orange chronograph displays contrasting against the black dial. It comes with two straps: one orange, one black, in an ultra-resistant composite material.

Design and Features:

  • Case: Steel. Diameter (10-4 o’clock): 42.2 mm. Height: 11.9 mm. Water resistant to 30 m. Screw-down crown. Sapphire crystal case back.
  • Dial: Black embossed, gold applied numerals with luminescent coating.
  • Movement: Self-winding mechanical movement. Caliber CH 28‑520 C/522. Flyback chronograph. Central chronograph hand. 60-minute counter. Date in an aperture.
  • Strap: Composite material, black (originally fitted). Composite material, orange (additional strap). Aquanaut fold-over clasp.
