The Richard Mille RM57-03 Tourbillon Sapphire Dragon is a limited-edition timepiece that masterfully combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. Limited to just 55 pieces, with 5 featuring sapphire cases, this watch is exclusively available in Asia.

Design and Features:

  • Case: Measuring 42.7mm in diameter, the case is crafted from white Carbon TPT, known for its lightweight and durable properties.
  • Dial: The openworked design showcases an intricately carved dragon, symbolizing strength and power in Chinese mythology. This dragon is meticulously sculpted from a single block of sapphire crystal, Integrated with carved Gold making the Body of a Dragon circulating the dial. A process that combines traditional engraving techniques with modern laser micro-cutting.
  • Movement: Manual-winding Calibre RM57-03, offering a power reserve of approximately 48 hours.
  • Strap: A black rubber strap complements the case, ensuring both comfort and a secure fit.
