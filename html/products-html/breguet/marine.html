The Breguet Marine Hora Mundi 5557 in white gold with a blue dial (Ref. 5557BB/YS/9WV) is a world-time marvel that reflects Breguet’s blend of nautical inspiration and technical mastery. It’s designed for global travelers who value elegance and mechanical ingenuity.

Housed in a 43.9mm 18-carat white gold case, the watch features a deep blue guilloché dial depicting a world map in relief. The instant-jump dual-time display allows the wearer to seamlessly switch between two time zones, synchronized with the date and day/night indicator.

At its heart is the self-winding Caliber 77F1, which integrates a unique memory mechanism for time zone changes, providing functionality without compromising aesthetic purity. The transparent sapphire caseback reveals exquisite finishing true to Breguet’s traditions.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 43.9mm
  • Case Material: 18k white gold
  • Dial: Blue guilloché with world map, dual-time with synchronized date and day/night indication
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: Breguet Caliber 77F1 (automatic)
  • Power Reserve: 55 hours
  • Frequency: 28,800 vph (4 Hz)
  • Strap: Blue rubber or leather strap with white gold deployant clasp
  • Water Resistance: 100 meters
  • Special Features: Instant-jump time zone mechanism with memory, day/night display, synchronized date
