The Breguet Tradition Tourbillon 7047 in platinum with a blue dial (Ref. 7047PT/1Y/9ZU) is a technical masterpiece that showcases Breguet’s enduring commitment to innovation and haute horology. This timepiece blends classic watchmaking elements with avant-garde aesthetics.

The watch features a 41mm platinum case and a stunning deep blue dial offset with exposed movement architecture, including a fusée-and-chain transmission system and a prominent tourbillon. The off-centered time display enhances its traditional yet futuristic appeal.

Powering this exceptional piece is the manually wound Caliber 569, offering an 80-hour power reserve and featuring a titanium tourbillon carriage. The sapphire caseback reveals the intricate mechanics, hand finishing, and traditional Breguet detailing.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 41mm
  • Case Material: Platinum
  • Dial: Blue with off-centered time, visible tourbillon, and fusée-and-chain mechanism
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: Breguet Caliber 569 (manual wind)
  • Power Reserve: 80 hours
  • Frequency: 18,000 vph (2.5 Hz)
  • Strap: Blue leather strap with platinum folding clasp
  • Water Resistance: 30 meters
  • Special Features: Titanium tourbillon, fusée-and-chain constant force mechanism
