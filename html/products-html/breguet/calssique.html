The Breguet Classique Quantième Perpétuel 5327 in rose gold (Ref. 5327BR/1E/9V6) is a sophisticated perpetual calendar watch that embodies Breguet’s timeless elegance and technical refinement. It masterfully displays the complexity of calendar mechanisms with classic styling.

This elegant timepiece is presented in a 39mm 18-carat rose gold case with finely fluted sides and welded lugs. The silvered gold dial is adorned with traditional hand-engraved guilloché and features Breguet’s signature blued steel hands and Roman numerals.

The watch is powered by the ultra-thin self-winding Caliber 502.3.P, featuring a perpetual calendar with day, date, month, leap year indication, and moon phase—accurately tracking the calendar without adjustment until the year 2100. The exhibition caseback reveals Breguet’s exceptional movement finishing.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 39mm
  • Case Material: 18k rose gold
  • Dial: Silvered gold with guilloché pattern, Roman numerals, blued Breguet hands
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: Breguet Caliber 502.3.P (automatic)
  • Power Reserve: 45 hours
  • Frequency: 21,600 vph (3 Hz)
  • Functions: Perpetual calendar (day, date, month, leap year), moon phase
  • Strap: Brown alligator leather with rose gold folding clasp
  • Water Resistance: 30 meters
