The Omega Seamaster Aqua Terra Worldtimer combines Omega's renowned precision with a sophisticated design, making it an excellent choice for those seeking a reliable and stylish timepiece.

Design and Features:

  • Case: A 43mm polished stainless steel case, providing a robust yet refined presence on the wrist.
  • Dial: A sun-brushed blue dial featuring vertical teak stripes and a circle of global destinations printed in red, enhancing its worldtimer functionality.
  • Movement: Equipped with Omega's Co-Axial Master Chronometer Calibre 8938, offering a 60-hour power reserve and resistance to magnetic fields up to 15,000 gauss.
  • Bracelet: Comes with a matching stainless steel bracelet, ensuring durability and comfort.
  • Water Resistance: Offers a water resistance of up to 150 meters, suitable for swimming and snorkeling.
