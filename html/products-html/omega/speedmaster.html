Design and Features:

  • Case: A 42mm stainless steel case with a brushed finish, featuring an asymmetrical design for enhanced protection of the winding crown and pushers. The case back is embossed with the Seahorse medallion, symbolizing Omega's maritime heritage.
  • Dial: A black step dial with applied Omega logo and the distinctive "dot over 90" on the anodized aluminum bezel ring, reflecting the classic Speedmaster design.
  • Movement: Powered by the Omega Co-Axial Master Chronometer Caliber 3861, a manual-winding movement offering a 50-hour power reserve and resistance to magnetic fields up to 15,000 gauss.
  • Crystal: Equipped with Hesalite crystal on the front, providing a vintage aesthetic reminiscent of the original Moonwatch models.
  • Bracelet: Comes with a stainless steel bracelet featuring a brushed finish and a one-sided deployant clasp, designed for comfort and security.
  • Water Resistance: Suitable for daily wear with a water resistance of up to 50 meters (165 feet).
