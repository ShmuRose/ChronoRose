The Omega Seamaster 300M 'No Time To Die' Edition is a special timepiece created in collaboration with Daniel Craig for his final portrayal of James Bond in the 2021 film "No Time To Die." This watch combines Omega's renowned craftsmanship with design elements reflecting Bond's character and military background.

Design and Features:

  • Case: A 42mm grade 2 titanium case, chosen for its lightweight and durable properties, suitable for the rigorous demands of espionage.
  • Dial: A brown anodized aluminum dial with cream luminous numerals and hands, featuring a broad arrow mark symbolizing military property.
  • Case Back: Engraved with military-style markings, including "0552" (Navy personnel), "923 7697" (dive watches), "A" (screw-down crown), "007" (James Bond), and "62" (reference to the first Bond film, "Dr. No").
  • Movement: Powered by Omega's Co-Axial Master Chronometer Caliber 8806, offering a 55-hour power reserve and resistance to magnetic fields up to 15,000 gauss.
  • Bracelet: Comes with a grey titanium mesh bracelet, providing a vintage aesthetic reminiscent of military-issued watches.
  • Water Resistance: Offers a water resistance of up to 300 meters (1,000 feet), suitable for diving and underwater missions.
