The Vacheron Constantin Overseas Chronograph (Ref. 5520V/210R-B966) is a refined sports watch that combines elegant aesthetics with robust chronograph functionality. Crafted entirely in 18k pink gold, this model stands out with its integrated bracelet, contemporary design, and technical sophistication.

Featuring a 42.5mm pink gold case and a stunning sunburst green lacquered dial, the watch includes three chronograph subdials, a date window at 4:30, and luminescent hands and markers. The hexagonal bezel and polished-brushed surfaces highlight its modern yet classic appeal.

It is powered by the in-house Caliber 5200, a column-wheel chronograph movement with a vertical clutch, visible through the sapphire caseback. The watch also boasts an interchangeable strap system, allowing the wearer to switch between the gold bracelet, green rubber, or green leather strap with ease.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 42.5mm
  • Case Material: 18k pink gold
  • Dial: Green sunburst with chronograph counters and date at 4:30
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: Vacheron Constantin Caliber 5200 (automatic)
  • Power Reserve: 52 hours
  • Frequency: 28,800 vph (4 Hz)
  • Functions: Hours, minutes, small seconds, chronograph, date
  • Straps: Interchangeable: pink gold bracelet, green rubber, and green leather strap
  • Water Resistance: 150 meters
