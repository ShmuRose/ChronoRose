The Vacheron Constantin Overseas Perpetual Calendar Ultra-Thin Skeleton (Ref. 4300V/220R-B642) is an extraordinary blend of elegance, complexity, and transparency. This openworked perpetual calendar is crafted entirely in 18k pink gold and showcases Vacheron Constantin’s technical mastery in an ultra-thin, sporty silhouette.

Housed in a 41.5mm rose gold case measuring just 8.1mm thick, the watch features a fully skeletonized dial and movement, offering an unobstructed view into the perpetual calendar’s intricate mechanisms. The transparent sapphire dial reveals day, date, month, moon phase, and leap year indications in a beautifully symmetrical layout.

At its core is the in-house automatic Caliber 1120 QPSQ/1, one of the thinnest perpetual calendar movements in existence. Its meticulous hand-finishing and openworked bridges are visible through the sapphire caseback and front, reinforcing the artistry within.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 41.5mm
  • Thickness: 8.1mm
  • Case Material: 18k pink gold
  • Dial: Skeletonized sapphire with perpetual calendar display (day, date, month, leap year, moon phase)
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: Vacheron Constantin Caliber 1120 QPSQ/1 (automatic)
  • Power Reserve: 40 hours
  • Frequency: 19,800 vph (2.75 Hz)
  • Functions: Hours, minutes, perpetual calendar with day, date, month, leap year, moon phase
  • Straps: Interchangeable: pink gold bracelet, leather strap, and rubber strap
  • Water Resistance: 50 meters
