The Vacheron Constantin Traditionnelle Tourbillon Perpetual Calendar (Ref. 8020T/000R-B653) exemplifies the pinnacle of classical haute horlogerie, combining two of the most prestigious complications—tourbillon and perpetual calendar—within a beautifully refined design.

Encased in 18k 5N pink gold, this 44mm timepiece showcases a sandblasted slate grey dial with an exquisite layout of calendar indicators, including day, date, month, leap year, and moon phase. The open tourbillon at 6 o'clock enhances both the watch’s technical character and visual harmony.

Inside beats the in-house automatic Caliber 2162 RQP, which features a peripheral rotor to allow an unobstructed view of the hand-finished movement through the sapphire caseback. Despite the complexity, the watch maintains an elegant profile and a power reserve of 72 hours.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 44mm
  • Thickness: 12.8mm
  • Case Material: 18k 5N pink gold
  • Dial: Sandblasted slate grey with perpetual calendar and open tourbillon
  • Crystal: Sapphire front and back
  • Movement: Vacheron Constantin Caliber 2162 RQP (automatic with peripheral rotor)
  • Power Reserve: 72 hours
  • Frequency: 18,000 vph (2.5 Hz)
  • Functions: Hours, minutes, tourbillon, perpetual calendar (day, date, month, leap year, moon phase)
  • Strap: Dark grey alligator leather with pink gold folding clasp
  • Water Resistance: 30 meters
