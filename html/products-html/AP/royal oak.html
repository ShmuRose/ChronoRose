The Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Selfwinding 15510ST.OO.1320ST.07 is a distinguished timepiece that combines the brand's signature design with modern enhancements. Introduced as a successor to the 50th Anniversary model, it offers a refined aesthetic and updated features.

Design and Features:

  • Case: 41 mm diameter, crafted from stainless steel. The case is water-resistant up to 50 meters, making it suitable for daily wear.
  • Dial: Black "Grande Tapisserie" pattern with white gold applied hour markers and Royal Oak hands with luminescent coating, ensuring readability in various lighting conditions.
  • Movement: Powered by the self-winding Caliber 4302, offering a power reserve of approximately 70 hours. This movement ensures precise timekeeping and reliability.
  • Bracelet: Stainless steel bracelet with an AP folding clasp, providing both comfort and security on the wrist.
