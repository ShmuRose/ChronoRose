The Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Perpetual Calendar "150th Anniversary" (Reference 26585XT.OO.1220XT.01) is a limited edition timepiece celebrating the brand's 150 years of fine watchmaking. This edition is limited to just 150 pieces, combining innovative materials with the iconic Royal Oak design. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Design and Features:

  • Case: 41mm diameter, crafted from titanium with a Bulk Metallic Glass (BMG) bezel and caseback frame. The case is water-resistant up to 20 meters.
  • Dial: Sapphire dial with blue accents, featuring sub-dials for day, date, month, and moon phase. The hour markers and hands are made of 18-carat white gold with luminescent coating.
  • Movement: Self-winding Calibre 5135, offering a 40-hour power reserve and operating at a frequency of 19,800 vibrations per hour. This movement features a perpetual calendar with week indication, day, date, astronomical moon, month, and leap year indication.
  • Bracelet: Titanium bracelet with BMG studs, featuring brushed surfaces with polished accents, secured with an AP folding clasp.
