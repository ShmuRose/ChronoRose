The Breitling Top Time Triumph is a special edition chronograph created in collaboration with British motorcycle manufacturer Triumph. This partnership celebrates the shared spirit of 1960s café racer culture, combining Breitling's Swiss watchmaking expertise with Triumph's iconic motorcycle design. It is limited in production and features a unique ice-blue dial, making it a sought-after timepiece for watch and motorcycle enthusiasts alike.

Design and Features:

  • Case: 41mm stainless steel case with a thickness of 14.27mm, providing a robust yet comfortable fit.
  • Dial: Ice blue "Zorro" dial with contrasting dark sub-dials, inspired by a rare 1970s Top Time model.
  • Movement: Equipped with Breitling's self-winding Caliber 23, offering a 48-hour power reserve and chronograph functions measuring up to 30 minutes.
  • Strap: Black calfskin leather strap with a racing-inspired design, secured with a stainless steel folding clasp.
  • Water Resistance: 30 meters, suitable for everyday wear.
