The Glashütte Original PanoMaticLunar is a distinguished timepiece that masterfully combines traditional German watchmaking with innovative design. Renowned for its asymmetric dial layout, it offers a harmonious blend of functionality and aesthetic appeal.

Design and Features:

  • Case: 40mm 18-karat rose gold case with a polished and satin-brushed finish, offering a refined and warm aesthetic.
  • Dial: Deep blue dial with a sunburst finish, featuring off-center hour and minute sub-dials, a Panorama Date display, and a moon phase indicator.
  • Movement:Powered by the in-house Caliber 90-02 automatic movement, providing a 42-hour power reserve and visible through the sapphire crystal case back.
  • Strap Options: Dark blue Louisiana alligator leather strap with a folding clasp, complementing the luxurious design
  • Water Resistance: Suitable for daily wear with a water resistance of up to 50 meters.
