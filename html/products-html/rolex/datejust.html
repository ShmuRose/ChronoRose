The Rolex Datejust Oyster Perpetual 36mm in White Gold, with Bright Blue Dial, Fluted bezel and a Jubilee bracelet.

The Rolex fluted bezel is a mark of distinction. Originally, the fluting of the Oyster bezel had a functional purpose: it served to screw the bezel onto the case helping to ensure the waterproofness of the watch. It was therefore identical to the fluting on the case back, which was also screwed onto the case for waterproofness, using specific Rolex tools. Over time, the fluting became an aesthetic element, a genuine Rolex signature feature. Today the fluted bezel is a mark of distinction, in gold on this Datejust 36.

Design and Features:

  • Case: Measuring 36mm in diameter, the case is crafted from White Rolesor - combination of Oystersteel and white gold, Making Rolex's unique White Gold.
  • Dial: Bright Blue Dial.
  • functions: Centre hour, minute and seconds hands. Instantaneous date with rapid setting. Stop-seconds for precise time setting
  • Movement: Self-winding Calibre 3235 by Rolex, offering a power reserve of approximately 70 hours. Precision of -2/+2 second a day.
  • crystal: Scratch-resistant sapphire, Cyclops lens over the date
  • Bracelet: Jubilee, five-piece links Style bracelet. Made of Oystersteel, with a Rolex Folding Oysterclasp with 5mm Easylink comfort extension link.
  • Water Resistance: Waterproof to 100 metres
