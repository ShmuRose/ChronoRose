The Rolex Day-Date Oyster Perpetual 40mm in 18ct Everose Gold, with Olive Green Dial, Fluted bezel and a President bracelet.

The Rolex fluted bezel is a mark of distinction. Originally, the fluting of the Oyster bezel had a functional purpose: it served to screw the bezel onto the case helping to ensure the waterproofness of the watch. It was therefore identical to the fluting on the case back, which was also screwed onto the case for waterproofness, using specific Rolex tools. Over time, the fluting became an aesthetic element, a genuine Rolex signature feature. Today the fluted bezel is a mark of distinction, in gold on this Day-Date 40.

Design and Features:

  • Case: Measuring 40mm in diameter, the case is crafted from 18ct Everose Gold.
  • Dial: Olive Green Dial.
  • functions: Centre hour, minute and seconds hands. Instantaneous day and date in apertures, secure rapid-setting. Stop-seconds for exact time setting.
  • Movement: Self-winding Calibre 3255 by Rolex, offering a power reserve of approximately 70 hours. Precision of -2/+2 second a day.
  • crystal: Scratch-resistant sapphire, Cyclops lens over the date
  • Bracelet: President Style bracelet. Made of 18ct Everose Gold, with a Rolex Concealed folding Crownclasp.
  • Water Resistance: Waterproof to 100 metres
