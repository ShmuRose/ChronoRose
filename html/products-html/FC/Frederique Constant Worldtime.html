The Frederique Constant Classic Worldtimer features a 42mm stainless steel case and a striking blue Clou de Paris-patterned dial with a detailed world map in the center. This timepiece offers world-time functionality with a rotating 24-hour disc and city ring, making it easy to track time in 24 different time zones.

At 6 o’clock, a date subdial adds daily practicality. The watch is powered by the in-house automatic FC-718 movement, offering a 42-hour power reserve. All complications—including world time and date— are adjustable via a single crown, ensuring smooth operation.

Fitted with a navy blue leather strap and protected by a convex sapphire crystal, this Worldtimer combines classic styling with travel-ready functionality.

Specifications:

  • Case Size: 42mm
  • Case Material: Stainless steel
  • Dial: Blue with Clou de Paris guilloché pattern and world map
  • Crystal: Convex sapphire
  • Movement: Frederique Constant FC-718 automatic
  • Functions: Hours, minutes, seconds, date, world time (24 cities), 24-hour day/night indication
  • Power Reserve: 42 hours
  • Strap: Blue leather with pin buckle
  • Water Resistance: 50 meters
  • Caseback: Exhibition (see-through sapphire)
