The IWC Portugieser Eternal Calendar is a remarkable timepiece that showcases IWC Schaffhausen's dedication to precision and innovation. Introduced in 2024, this watch features a secular perpetual calendar and an extraordinarily accurate moon phase display.

Design and Features:

  • Case: A 44.4mm platinum case with a transparent glass dial, allowing a clear view of the intricate movement inside.
  • Movement: The IWC-manufactured Caliber 52640 automatic movement, providing a 7-day power reserve.
  • Perpetual Calendar: Adjusts for leap years and months with varying lengths, ensuring accurate date display without manual correction.
  • Moon Phase Display: Features a Double Moon™ display with an accuracy that will deviate by only one day after 45 million years.
